I wish to create an algorithm better than page rank algorithm and become the next 'genius' like Sergey Brin was. You could even say the thought of my future motivates me to work even harder every day. My passion for software is immeasurable and at the heart of this captivation is the endless problem-solving capabilities which is the core of software engineer.
